Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

What types of services do you provide?
Builderz offers construction, renovation, interior fit-outs, maintenance and design coordination services for residential and commercial clients.
How do you prepare project estimates?
We perform a site visit, define scope and material selections, then provide a detailed itemized estimate and timeline.
Can you work with my architect or designer?
Yes — we collaborate closely with client architects and designers to deliver the intended design while maintaining buildability and cost control.
What is your project timeline?
Timelines depend on scope; we provide a project schedule during quoting and update it through construction with milestone tracking.
What warranty and aftercare do you provide?
We provide a 1-year workmanship warranty and assist with product warranties; maintenance packages can be arranged for ongoing care.
Do you handle permits and inspections?
Yes — Builderz manages permit submissions, coordinates inspections and ensures compliance with local building regulations.
How do you ensure quality on site?
We use milestone inspections, material approvals and qualified supervisors to maintain consistent quality throughout construction.
Can you provide references?
Yes — we are happy to provide project portfolios, client references and photographs of completed work on request.
Do you provide maintenance and after-sales support?
Yes — return visits, repairs and maintenance contracts are available to keep your property in top condition after handover.
How do I request a quote or schedule a site visit?
Contact our office by phone or use the contact form to schedule a free site visit — we will follow up with a detailed proposal.
