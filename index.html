Welcome to Builderz

25 Years Experience

Builderz is a full-service construction company based in Ahmednagar. With over 25 years of experience we deliver residential, commercial and public projects with a focus on safety, quality and local expertise.

Our team blends traditional craftsmanship with modern construction methods. We manage design, permitting, site management and finishing to provide a single accountable partner from concept through handover.
